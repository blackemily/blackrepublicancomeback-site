The main skill areas inside this school
These are the core digital arenas that open doors: from writing pages and emails to building simple sites, understanding SEO, and setting up basic online offers.
Coding & Web Development
Learn how the web is actually built

You’ll work through HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript ideas in plain language, with help applying them to simple pages and practical layouts.

  • What HTML, CSS, and JS do — and how they fit together.
  • Build simple, clean pages instead of copy-pasting chaos.
  • Understand enough to talk to developers and not be lost.
SEO, Content & Digital Marketing
Get found and understood online

You’ll learn how search engines, content, and basic funnels work, plus how to write pages and posts that attract the right people instead of everyone.

  • SEO basics: search intent, keywords, on-page structure.
  • Write content that’s clear, useful, and discoverable.
  • Understand simple funnels: lead, nurture, and offer.
Copywriting & Social Media
Words that move people to act

You’ll practice writing headlines, offers, and short posts that are direct, honest, and focused on real value — not fake urgency and manipulative tricks.

  • Offer-focused copy instead of empty slogans.
  • Social media posts that point somewhere useful.
  • Emails that respect the reader and still sell.
AI, Automation & Simple Online Offers
Use tools instead of being used by them

You’ll learn how to use AI and basic automations to save time, not to replace thinking — and how to turn skills into simple offers or micro-services.

  • Prompt basics and how to structure AI conversations.
  • Simple automations (email, forms, basic workflows).
  • Turn skills into clear “I do this for X people” offers.
Core tracks inside this school
You don’t have to take everything. Pick one track that lines up with your immediate reality, and work through it with the tutor until you can actually ship something.
Four focused learning tracks
Each track is designed to lead to visible output: a page, a sequence, a basic site, or a simple starter offer. The tutor can adapt the pace to where you are now.
  • Track 1 – Web Foundations
    Learn HTML and CSS by building a handful of simple, clean pages you could proudly show a real person — not just tutorial exercises.
  • Track 2 – SEO & Content Essentials
    Map a topic, choose search terms, outline pages, and write content designed to attract the right searchers over time.
  • Track 3 – Copy, Email & Social Basics
    Write a simple offer page, a short email sequence, and a set of social posts that all point in the same direction.
  • Track 4 – AI & Starter Offers
    Use AI to speed up drafting and planning, then design one realistic micro-offer you could actually fulfill for a client or employer.
How this fits the larger comeback

A comeback in thinking is powerful. A comeback in earning power is too. This school exists so that more people can participate in the digital economy as creators, helpers, and builders — not just as users and targets.

You might use these skills to support a local business, to become the “tech person” at your job, or to launch something small of your own. What matters is that you stop feeling locked out of how modern work actually happens.

The point is not hype. The point is competence.
