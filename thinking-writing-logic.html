School 1: Thinking, Writing & Logic

This is the foundation of the entire comeback. Clear writing, clear thinking, and clear reasoning. If your mind is sharp, the rest of your life becomes easier to build.

How This School Works
Pick a tutor, learn at your own pace, practice whenever you want.

School 1 is designed like a private tutor for your mind. Each GPT in this school helps you learn, revise, practice, and grow. You can start anywhere — writing, logic, reading, or philosophy — and switch between them anytime.

The more you practice explaining things in your own words, the quicker your thinking sharpens. You’ll notice the difference in how you speak, write, decide, and evaluate information.

No schedules, no deadlines, and no grades. Just clarity, practice, and progress. When you’re ready, move on to School 2 (History, Economics & Civic Truth) or jump ahead to whichever part of your comeback needs work.

Certificates
Print a reminder of the work you’ve put in.

After you complete a defined path (Writing, Logic, Philosophy, or Reading), you can print a mini-certificate for each area. If you complete all major areas of School 1, you’ll earn the School 1 Certificate of Clarity & Reasoning.

None of this is accredited, but the point isn’t a credential — the point is that your thinking is sharper than it was when you started.
