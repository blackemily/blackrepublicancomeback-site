The theory behind the skilled trades

Not everyone will work with their hands — but everyone should understand how the systems around them function. This school teaches the underlying theory behind the major trades: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, auto repair, welding, blueprint reading, and job‑site safety.

What You’ll Learn
Concept-first explanations designed for beginners, job seekers, and curious adults.
HVAC Theory
  • Airflow, pressure, and ductwork logic
  • Refrigeration cycle in plain language
  • Heat transfer and system components
Plumbing Theory
  • Water supply & pressure principles
  • Drainage, venting, traps, slopes
  • Pipes, fittings, and system materials
Electrical Basics
  • Voltage, current, resistance, circuits
  • Panels, breakers, and household logic
  • Safety & grounding principles
Auto Repair Theory
  • Engine, drivetrain, cooling systems
  • Fuel-air-spark fundamentals
  • Troubleshooting and diagnostic thinking
Welding Fundamentals
  • MIG, TIG, Stick — core concepts
  • Heat, metals, joints, distortion
  • Weld symbols & reading expectations
Blueprint Reading
  • Symbols, scales & technical drawings
  • Floor plans, elevations, sections
  • How different trades use the same plans
Safety & OSHA Prep
  • PPE, hazards, job‑site safety
  • OSHA rules & compliance logic
  • Prep for OSHA‑10 / OSHA‑30 concepts
